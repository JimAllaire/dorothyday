Custom Search


Making Efficient Queries

Simple earch
Start with a single name, place, concept: Peter Maurin, pacifism, hospitality.
Add relevant words
Typing pacifism poverty returns documents where pacifism AND poverty are present, narrowing a simple search.
Search for exact word or phrase
Put quotes around a word or search term for an exact phrase in an exact order: "works of mercy".
Either word query
If you want to search for pages that may have just one of several words, include OR (capitalized) between the words: poverty OR pacifism.
Don't worry about cases
Search isn't case sensitive: peter maurin and Peter Maurin return the same results.
